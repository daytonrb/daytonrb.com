Dayton.rb is a monthly gathering of local technologists who meet to talk about all things Ruby.



Next Meeting: Tuesday, Jan 22 2013 at 6:00PM

About

We are group of programmers, students, and technology enthusiasts who are excited about the Ruby programming language and the Ruby on Rails web framework.

We meet on the fourth Tuesday of every month to contribute to open source, work on personal projects, and present topics related to Ruby. All skill levels are welcome!

Why Dayton.rb?

We are excited with the growing Ruby crowd here in Dayton. The Ruby community is known for their sharing and collaboration, and we want to take an active role.

Our goal is to create an environment where rubyist of all skill levels can learn and share ideas.
