Attention Rubyist: Dayton Ohio knows what ruby is and how to use it!

A Southern Ohio based group of local developers who meet to talk about Ruby and lean new things.

About

You can start by signing up for email updates. Then check out the fun people who thought this Ruby Group would be a good idea. Then be sure to clear your calender for the next meeting.

Why?

We are excited that you have come here to learn how to get involved in the growing Ruby community here in Dayton. If you have any questions hit us up on our twitter page or fill out the contact form below.

Where We Meet

Today we meet on the third tuesday of every month to discuss and hack on Ruby code and strive to keep to the principle of less slides more code.
